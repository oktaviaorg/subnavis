Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an API key for the free tier?

No! The Rebel tier works without authentication. Just start making requests. For higher limits, you'll get an API key when you subscribe.

How do I pay with TAO?

Select your plan and choose "Pay with TAO" at checkout. You'll receive a wallet address to send the payment. After confirmation, your API key is activated within 24 hours.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

You'll receive a 429 error. Upgrade your plan or wait until the next day when your limit resets. We never charge overage fees.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
