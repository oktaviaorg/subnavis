How to Stake TAO

üìñ 8 min read ‚Ä¢ Earn 15-25% APY

Staking TAO is the easiest way to earn passive income on Bittensor. By delegating your tokens to validators, you help secure the network and earn 15-25% APY in rewards ‚Äî without running any hardware.

üí∞ What You'll Earn

With 10 TAO staked at 20% APY, you'd earn approximately 2 TAO per year (~$560 at current prices). Your rewards compound automatically.

üìã What You Need

  • A TAO wallet (Polkadot.js, Bittensor CLI, or compatible wallet)
  • TAO tokens to stake (minimum varies by validator)
  • 5 minutes of your time

üîÑ Step-by-Step Guide

1Get a TAO Wallet

If you don't have one yet, create a wallet using Polkadot.js extension or the Bittensor CLI. Your wallet address starts with "5".

2Buy TAO Tokens

Purchase TAO on exchanges like MEXC, Gate.io, KuCoin, or Bitget. Transfer to your wallet address.

3Choose a Validator

Pick a validator based on APY, reputation, and uptime. See our recommendations below. Each subnet has different validators.

4Delegate Your TAO

Use the Bittensor CLI or a staking interface to delegate. Command: btcli stake add --wallet.name YOUR_WALLET --subtensor.network finney

5Earn Rewards

Rewards are distributed every ~12 seconds (tempo). They automatically compound ‚Äî no action needed!

üîì Unstaking Your TAO

Need your TAO back? You can unstake anytime, but there's a ~7 day cooldown before tokens return to your wallet. Plan ahead!

Command: btcli stake remove --wallet.name YOUR_WALLET

‚ùì FAQ

What's the minimum to stake?

There's no network minimum, but some validators set their own minimums (usually 0.1-1 TAO). Gas fees are minimal.

Are my tokens safe?

Yes! Staked TAO never leaves your wallet. You're delegating voting power, not custody. You can unstake anytime.

How often do I get rewards?

Rewards are distributed every tempo (~12 seconds on most subnets). They compound automatically.

Can I stake on multiple subnets?

Yes! You can split your TAO across multiple validators and subnets. This is actually recommended for diversification.
